About the Game

You have been cast into another world as a Dark Lord, feared, hunted, and mortal. But you carry one gift no enemy can take: the power to be reborn.


Each life is a branching path. Every death teaches. Choose how you grow through dark magic, loyal minions, or monstrous evolution. The world will not yield easily. Resources are scarce, time runs short, and each route demands real strategy.


This is not a game of patience. It is a game of mastery.

Key Features
ℜ

Reincarnation Loop

Die, remember, return. Each rebirth carries forward the wisdom of your past life, opening new paths that were impossible before.

𝔅

Branching Path Tree

Every decision forks the road. All choices are visualized as a branching tree, plan your run, or discover by experimentation.

𝔎

Genuine Challenge

Tight time limits, scarce resources, and layered strategy. Multiple endings require real planning, Every second counts.

𝔐

Multiple Endings

Several distinct endings and hidden secrets are woven through every chapter. Finding them all demands mastery of every path.

𝔓

Prestige System

Convert passive XP into "Chapter Memories", permanent boosts that carry across chapters and accelerate future runs.

𝔇

Three Dark Paths

Master dark magic, build an army of minions, or evolve into a terrifying creature. Each path opens unique strategies and endings.
